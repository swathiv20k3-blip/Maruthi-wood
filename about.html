About Us

  • Started in 1970, Maruthi Woods has grown into a leading timber supplier and exporter the globe.
  • Operating from 4 major branches: Sengottai, Trichy, Chennai, and Tuticorin.
  • Commitment to quality, sustainability, and global standards.
  • Decades of experience in sourcing and delivering a wide range of timber materials.
